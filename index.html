LEVEL 3: THE MAGIC PROMPT

HOW MIGHT WE (HMW)

WHAT IS IT? →

The 'How Might We' method turns your mapped gaps and resources into an actionable challenge, framing the problem for creative ideation.

WHY "WE" NOT "I"? →

Because no one closes their gaps alone. Every breakthrough needs a collaborator, a mentor, or someone you haven't met yet. Asking "we" keeps the door open.

THE FORMULA
HOW MIGHT WE ACHIEVE [GOAL X]
USING [RESOURCES Y]
DESPITE [GAPS Z] ?

💡 You can write more than one HMW question — explore different combinations!
